How do you value money over time?

This survey explores your time preferences — how you weigh receiving money now versus waiting for a larger amount later.

You will be asked 10 questions. Each question presents a choice between some amount of money right now and $1,000 in the future. Your job is to enter the minimum dollar amount right now that you would accept instead of waiting.

There are no right or wrong answers — just respond honestly. The survey takes about 2 minutes.
